Preparation of Consolidated
Final Accounts

Consolidated financial statements present the financial information of a parent and its subsidiaries as if they were a single economic entity. These statements include assets, liabilities, equity, income, expenses, and cash flows. By presenting this information in a consolidated manner, stakeholders can obtain a better understanding of the financial position and performance of the entire group, rather than just the parent company. The IFRS ensures that consolidated financial statements are prepared accurately and consistently, providing transparency and reliability to investors and other stakeholders.

T10 Certificate

At the conclusion of the accounting year, we compile a comprehensive summary of all employee details regarding tax deductions made throughout the year. For employees who have requested a T10 Certificate, we issue a certificate of tax deduction by the end of the financial year. This certificate serves as an important document that employees can submit to the Inland Revenue Department to fulfill their personal income tax requirements. Our timely provision of these certificates ensures smooth tax filing processes for employees, facilitating compliance with tax regulations.
